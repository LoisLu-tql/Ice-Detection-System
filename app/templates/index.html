Welcome to Sea Ice Detector

Nowadays, sea ice detection problem becomes more and more important with the climate change. Polar ice detection can also be used to provide accurate sea ice information to ensure the safety of the voyage, study the Arctic ecological environment, forecast sea ice disasters, and develop marine fisheries. Artificial intelligence technology continues to develop and has played an indispensable role in many fields. We use the semantic segmentation technology to predict sea ice in the given pictures.

A Platform that Integrates Multiple Models

In our website, you can use multiple models we trained to predict sea ice. The models are still updating. We are also very pleased if you are willing to upload your model.

Accurate, Effective and Exoteric

We aim to build a high-accuracy predicting platform. We are also happy to communicate and study more about sea ice detection.

Based on massive data

Models are trained by hundreds and thousands of images. Some models are trained by SAR data while some models are trained by MODIS data. SAR is a type of active data collection where a sensor produces its own energy and then records the amount of that energy reflected back after interacting with the Earth. The MODIS Data section contains everything from ATBDs to Product Descriptions to Product ordering information, including from Direct Broadcast data providers.

Application of semantic segmentation

Semantic segmentation, or image segmentation, is the task of clustering parts of an image together which belong to the same object class. It is a form of pixel-level prediction because each pixel in an image is classified according to a category. The initial semantic segmentation technology uses the deconvolution layer for up-sampling to classify every pixel in the picture.

MTV Pattern

The Model provides a definition of how the data formats as coming from the view so, it stores in the database. The View in this MTV architecture is formatting the data via the model. In turn, it communicates to the database and that data which transfer to the template for viewing. Template’s main goal is to keep everything that browser renders.
